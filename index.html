Acknowledgement & Gratitude

When cohort 59 first began class we were all strangers who came from very different walks of life. Our work & life experience includes that of medical professions, military veterans, musicians, graphic designers, food service professionals, salespeople, & more. With the help from the instructors mentioned below, those seemingly large gaps between us disappeared because now we all share something in common - a curiosity to learn how to be software developers.

Thank you to our amazing instructors; we couldn't have done it without you! Val, Dakota, Ross, Josh, Taylor, & Devin -- you were all a great resource into the world of software development & we are so grateful for the positive learning environment you cultivated. Thank you for the wealth of knowledge you bestowed upon us & we will forever look back at our time here fondly.

Thank you to the career development team, Ashley Canino, Michael Frieh, & Marla Lamont -- for all the feedback & advice, for helping us connect with various senior developers, thank you!!

Thank you to Nashville Software School -- for giving us a chance to learn, grow, and create.

Finally, to our Class Website Committee: Maria Q Manasala, Renee Doll, Justin Sweeten, Abigayle Jones, Reggie L Williams Jr, & Sarah Beth Langley -- thank you for taking the time & effort to work on this project outside of class!
